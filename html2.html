Here are some of the best science museums around the world:

In the United States

Museum of Science Boston: Features a giant Rube Goldberg machine and a wing dedicated to the science of engineering

Exploratorium: Offers interactive exhibits focused on science, art, and human perception, including the famous "Tactile Dome"

National Air and Space Museum: Home to historic aircraft and spacecraft, including the Wright 1903 Flyer and the Spirit of St. Louis

Museum of Science and Industry: Features interactive exhibits, including a six-foot floating globe and a digital image creator

In India

Visvesvaraya Industrial and Technological Museum: Offers interactive exhibits and live demonstrations, located in Cubbon Park

Science City: A fun science museum with interactive exhibits and activities for kids

Regional Science Centre & Planetarium: Features a planetarium and interactive science exhibits
